About

Hey, I’m Hubo

I build things that move, blink, and occasionally behave.

I’m an ECE undergrad in the UK, into robotics / control / embedded systems and the kind of ML that still works when sensors are noisy and reality refuses to be clean. If it needs debugging with a logic analyzer at 2am, I’m probably interested.

What I’m into

  • Robots & control: gait stuff, motion generation, “why is it drifting?” type problems
  • Applied ML: learning + optimisation under constraints (not just leaderboard chasing)
  • Embedded & digital: hardware bring-up, firmware, performance-minded implementation
